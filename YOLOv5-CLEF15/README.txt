This file contains YOLOv5 model, training weights and datasets.

clf15_org —————  the original cleaned lifeCLEF-2015 dataset
clf15_retinex ———— lifeCLEF-2015 dataset after applied autoMSRCR
clf15_balance ———— lifeCLEF-2015 dataset after applied autoMSRCR and data balance
clf15_aug ———— lifeCLEF-2015 dataset after applied autoMSRCR and Gaussian blur

clf15_weight ———— best weight of different strategies

runs/train ———— training files of different strategies 
runs/test -—— test results of different strategies 


Other documents are open-source YOLOv5 model that provided by YOLOv5 official.
